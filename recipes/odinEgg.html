Recipe: The Outstanding Odin Egg

Nothing ordinary about this egg...

Premium one-of-a-kind egg sourced from the depths of The Odin Caves.
Plucked from the nests of Odin bats.
These eggs taste like nothing you've ever had before.

How To Craft:

Ingredients

  • One (1) Odin bat egg
  • Stove, or other source of fire
  • Two (2) plates
  1. Obtain the Odin bat egg (don't have an Odin bat egg? stop immediately and choose another recipe.)
  2. Crack the Odin bat egg
  3. Put a plate over fire
  4. Plop egg onto plate
  5. Allow egg to simmer for 19 minutes
  6. Once it's been 19 minutes and you've absolutely seared the plate, there should be a strong smell
  7. That smell means done-zo
  8. Turn off the fire, place delicately cooked egg onto a fresh plate, throw old plate away because you ruined it
  9. Voila, serve warm and enjoy
